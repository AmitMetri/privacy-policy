Color Book Application

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Color Book ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our approach to user privacy and the minimal permissions we request.

Important: Color Book does not collect, store, or share any personal information or device data. We respect your privacy and have designed our app with privacy in mind.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or device data from our users.

The Color Book app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit, collect, or store any user data, including but not limited to:

  • No personal identification information
  • No device information (device type, model, manufacturer, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No IP addresses or location data
  • No browsing history or app usage patterns
  • No colored artwork or saved images

Permissions:

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Permission: Required to save your completed coloring pages to your device's gallery and to share your artwork. All images remain stored locally on your device only.
  • File Provider: Used to enable sharing of your colored images with other apps (such as messaging or social media apps) from within Color Book. This permission does not enable us to collect or transmit any user data.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and do not enable us to collect, transmit, or access any personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, process, or analyze any user data.

The permissions requested are used solely to:

  • Save your completed coloring artwork to your device's local storage
  • Enable sharing of your colored images with other apps on your device
  • Display coloring pages and provide coloring functionality within the app

All coloring pages, artwork, and preferences are stored locally on your device and never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them using your device's sharing functionality.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Color Book does not:

  • Sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties
  • Share data with analytics providers
  • Use advertising networks that track users
  • Integrate with any third-party services that collect user data
  • Transmit any information to our servers or any external servers
  • Upload or store your colored artwork to any cloud service

When you choose to share your colored artwork using the share functionality, the image is shared directly from your device to the app you select (such as messaging or social media apps) without passing through our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Color Book operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection. All coloring functionality and data storage occurs locally on your device.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any user data, there is no user data at risk. All app functionality, including coloring, saving artwork, and viewing your creations, occurs locally on your device, ensuring your privacy is fully protected.

Your colored artwork and preferences are stored on your device using standard Android storage practices. The security of this locally stored data is managed by your device's operating system and your device security settings.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data because we do not collect any user data. All coloring pages, completed artwork, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Your colored images remain on your device until you choose to delete them using your device's file manager or gallery app.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect any personal information:

  • There is no data to access, correct, or delete from our systems
  • You have complete control over the app and all stored artwork on your device
  • You can delete any saved coloring images directly from your device's gallery
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, and no data will remain with us
  • You can revoke storage permissions at any time through your device settings (note: this may limit the app's ability to save your artwork)

9. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Color Book is specifically designed as a family-friendly coloring application suitable for children. Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children, there are no privacy concerns related to children's data.

Color Book complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design, as we do not collect, use, or disclose personal information from children.
